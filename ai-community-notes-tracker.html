Helpful Community Notes on X, by type of author

Methodology

Notes are classified into three author types. AI Contributors are notes written by users whose enrollmentState is apiEarnedIn in the userEnrollment file. AI + Human are collaborative notes (identified by isCollaborativeNote in the notes file), which are initiated by AI at the request of high-impact users and then refined by human contributors. Human covers all remaining notes. A note is counted as "helpful" if its currentStatus in the noteStatusHistory file is CURRENTLY_RATED_HELPFUL. The AI Share line includes both AI Contributors and AI + Human notes. The date used is the note's creation date. Data source: X Community Notes public data.
