Methodology and caveats
Data source: X Community Notes public data. Please note X shares data with a lag and sometimes inconsistently. This chart is updated at least once a week, provided data is available.

Notes are classified into three author types. AI are notes written by users whose enrollmentState is apiEarnedIn in the userEnrollment file. AI + Human are collaborative notes (identified by isCollaborativeNote in the notes file), which are initiated by AI at the request of high-impact users and then refined by human contributors. Human covers all remaining notes. A note is counted as "helpful" if its currentStatus in the noteStatusHistory file is CURRENTLY_RATED_HELPFUL. The AI Share line includes both AI and AI + Human notes. The date used is the note's creation date.

The most recent day in the dataset is excluded from statistics and chart data as it is typically incomplete; peak AI share is calculated only from complete days. Please note that the last day will typically see a dip in helpful notes compared to the trend because it takes some time for notes to reach that status.
